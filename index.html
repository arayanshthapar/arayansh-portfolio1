About

Arayansh Thapar is an indie singer-songwriter and guitarist blending modern soul with classic blues and Indian roots. From The Last Leaf and The Trio to his latest project Arayansh & The Phoenixes, Arayansh’s music explores emotion and storytelling, now streaming everywhere.
