Bands & Collaborations

The Trio

I had a lot of fun playing with The Trio as the lead guitarist, alongside Donny Berry on bass and Shobhit Chaudhary on drums. Performing with them gave me the space to explore and become more flexible as a musician, which helped me find the raw, authentic sound I have today. Both Donny and Shobhit are still part of my current band, Arayansh and the Phoenixes.

Arayansh and the Phoenixes

We continue to push our sound with original music and dynamic live shows. The collaborative energy we built as The Trio is now at the heart of every performance with the Phoenixes.

The Last Leaf (India)

As lead guitarist for The Last Leaf, I performed in the Battle of the Bands in India. It was an amazing experience that pushed my skills on stage and in studio sessions, and introduced me to the thriving indie music scene back home.

Jamnetwork & Acoustic Open Mics

I’ve collaborated with various musicians through Jamnetwork, participating in jams, open mics, and acoustic gigs. These collaborations have given me the chance to experiment with new genres and sounds, and many of my best memories come from these spontaneous musical sessions.

About

Arayansh Thapar is a singer-songwriter, guitarist, and performer whose music blends blues, rock, and pop. Known for emotive lyrics and expressive guitar work, Arayansh has played across India and Canada, sharing stages with bands like The Last Leaf and leading The Trio before forming his current group, Arayansh and the Phoenixes. His latest EP “Emotions” explores raw human experience, inspired by artists like John Mayer and B.B. King. Whether on stage or at acoustic open mics, Arayansh’s performances are about connecting with listeners and creating honest, unforgettable moments.
